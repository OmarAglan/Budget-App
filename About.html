Our Mission

Simplifying Financial Freedom

We believe that managing your money shouldn't be a chore. It should be intuitive, private, and empowering.

Why we built this?

Traditional finance apps are often cluttered, require sensitive bank connections, or sell your data. We wanted to build something different.

100% Offline Capable: Works without internet.
Privacy First: Your data stays in your browser.
Open Source: Transparent and community-driven.
Accessible Design

Built with modern accessibility standards to ensure everyone can manage their finances effectively.

Zero Data Collection

We don't track you. No cookies, no analytics, no external servers. Just you and your budget.

Lightning Fast

Optimized performance using vanilla JavaScript and local storage for instant interactions.
